Are you a parent that might not know how to react to your child coming out? Or are you someone who just wants to be informed on the experiences of the LGBTQ+ community? Join this family in the interactive comic “Pride (And How To React To Your Child Coming Out)”!

The plot tells the story of a young male who happily embraces his identity as a homosexual man; however, he is conflicted because he still does not know how to tell his parents. After finding a poster for an LGBTQ+ community party in his bedroom, the parents confront their son and demand to know the truth. The user is given the choice to play in either the boy's or the parents' perspective, and your choices will affect how the plot will play out. In addition, there are some hidden sound effects that can be played throughout the comic when clicked on, so don’t do to the next panel unless you’ve found all!

Will you be conservative or be accepting of your son? Will you choose to tell the truth or lie about your identity? We shall find out! Well, what are you waiting for? Go to the Home page and press “Start Comic” now!
